Biography

Zhengru Fang received his B.S. degree (Hons.) in electronics and information engineering from the Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, in 2019 and received his M.S. degree (Hons.) from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2022.

Under the supervision of Prof. Yuguang "Michael" Fang, he is pursuing Doctor of Philosophy in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, sponsored by Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS). His research interests include collaborative perception, V2X, age of information, and mobile edge computing.

He has been serving as a Reviewer for IEEE JSAC, IEEE IoTJ, IEEE TVT, IEEE WCL, IEEE CL, IEEE VTM, etc.
